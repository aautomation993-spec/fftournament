Tournament Rules

1. Fair Play

All participants must adhere to fair play principles. Cheating, hacking, or any form of unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.

2. Registration

Players must register with accurate information. False information may lead to disqualification.

3. Match Conduct

Respect all players and officials. Toxic behavior, harassment, or unsportsmanlike conduct will result in penalties.

4. Technical Requirements

Ensure stable internet connection and compatible device. Technical issues do not excuse rule violations.

5. Prize Distribution

Prizes will be distributed within 7 days after the tournament ends. Winners must provide valid payment details.
